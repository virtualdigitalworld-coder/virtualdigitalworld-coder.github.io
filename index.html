Our Story

A young Nepali studio that found its identity in engraving.

A group of passionate young individuals came together with a vision to create something meaningful in Nepal. What began as a small initiative rooted in creativity and craftsmanship soon found its identity in engraving — crafting beautiful, personalised designs on wood, glass and acrylic.

Craftsmanship

Meaningful pieces from quality woods — from affordable Karam to premium Sissoo and Walnut, balancing durability, elegance and value.

Our Expertise

Wood, bamboo, glass and acrylic products — crafted both by hand and with advanced machinery, for custom gifts and corporate solutions.

Sustainability

“Engraving with Love” is our promise to create eco-friendly products that give back to the community and environment.
